# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Indian families, NRIs booking care for their parents in India, working professionals, and seniors needing accessible healthcare. They value trust, speed, and reliability.

## Product Purpose
Health Metro connects patients with certified labs, trained professionals, and specialist doctors through a single platform. It offers services ranging from diagnostics (HM MOVE), home sample collection (HM EASY), doctor consultations (HM TRUST), NRI care (HM RELY), digital health records (HM OHR), and clinical research (HM CLIN).

## Brand Personality
Trusted, professional, fast, reliable, modern, accessible.

## Anti-references
SaaS-cream designs, confusing medical portals, generic startup templates. Should feel like a premium, trustworthy medical service.

## Design Principles
- Patient First: Clear, accessible, and simple.
- Trusted & Safe: Convey reliability and security.
- Fast & Reliable: Performance and ease of use.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Must be accessible to seniors. Clear typography, high contrast, readable tooltips, keyboard accessible.
